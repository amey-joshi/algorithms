Given a string, find the length of the longest substring without
repeating characters.

Example: "abcabcbb". "abc" is longest substring without repeating
characters.

Example: "bbbbbbb". "b" is longest substring without repeating
characters.

Example: "pwwkew". "wke" is longest substring without repeating
characters.

Source: https://leetcode.com/problems/longest-substring-without-repeating-characters/
